Mississippi Roast

Serve egg noodles or mashed potato with this tender, 5-ingredient Mississippi chuck roast. Cook on
low for 8 hours before enjoying this bold and buttery beef entree that gets its unique flavor from
pepperoncini peppers.

Ingredients

  • 1 (4 pound) beef chuck roast
  • Â¼ cup butter
  • 5 pepperoncini peppers
  • 1 (1 ounce) packet ranch dressing mix
  • 1 (1 ounce) packet dry au jus mix

Steps

  1. Place roast in a slow cooker. Form a pocket in the top of the roast and place
    butter, pepperoncini peppers, ranch dressing mix, and au jus mix in the pocket.

  2. Cook on Low for 8 hours.
